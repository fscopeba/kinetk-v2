Our Money-Back Guarantee

We're so confident in KINETK that we back it with a no-questions-asked guarantee. Your success is our success.

What We Guarantee

We guarantee that with KINETK, you will either:

  • ✓ Pass ATS screening on resumes you optimize with our platform, or
  • ✓ Land at least 1 interview within 30 days of submitting optimized resumes

If neither happens, we'll refund 100% of your payment. No questions asked. No fine print.

What Qualifies

Covered Plans

This guarantee applies to:

  • Standard Package: Full coverage
  • Premium Package: Full coverage
  • Basic Package: Free rewrite guarantee only (see below)

Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for this guarantee, you must:

  • Use KINETK to optimize your resume before applying
  • Apply to at least 10 positions using your optimized resume within the 30-day period
  • Apply to roles that match your qualifications and experience level
  • Use the resume file generated by KINETK (no additional edits that modify formatting)
  • Document your applications with dates and company names

What Doesn't Qualify

The guarantee does not cover:

  • Applying to positions you are significantly unqualified for
  • Failure to submit applications within the 30-day window
  • Substantial changes to resume formatting after optimization
  • Applications to positions closed before you submitted

How to Claim Your Refund

If you don't pass ATS or land an interview within 30 days, claiming your refund is simple:

Step 1: Document Your Applications

Gather proof of at least 10 applications submitted with your KINETK-optimized resume. This includes:

  • Company names and job titles
  • Application dates
  • Screenshots or confirmation emails showing submission

Step 2: Contact Support

Email support@kinetk.io with the subject line "Guarantee Claim" and include:

  • Your KINETK account email
  • Your documentation of applications (see above)
  • A brief note explaining the outcome

Step 3: Verification & Refund

Our team will review your claim within 5 business days. If you qualify, we'll issue a full refund to your original payment method within 7 business days.
