Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

What We Collect

When you use KINETK services, we collect the following information:

  • Resume Content: The resume you upload or create for analysis
  • Account Information: Email address, name, and account credentials
  • Job Descriptions: Job postings you submit for analysis
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform (features accessed, time spent, etc.)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide ATS analysis, resume optimization, and other KINETK services
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, process payments, and provide customer support
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our products and features
  • Communication: To send service updates, security alerts, and marketing emails (which you can opt out of)
  • Analytics: To understand how our platform is being used and identify trends

Data Security

Your data security is our top priority. Here's how we protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from KINETK is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Your resume and personal information are stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Access Controls: Only authorized KINETK employees with a legitimate business need have access to your data
  • Automatic Deletion: Resume files and job descriptions are automatically deleted 30 days after analysis completion unless you request longer storage
  • Compliance: We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information or resume content to third parties. KINETK operates independently and does not share your data with recruiters, employers, or other external parties.

We only use trusted service providers (such as cloud hosting and payment processors) who are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@kinetk.io
  • Mail: KINETK, Privacy Team
  • Contact Page: Contact Us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above.
